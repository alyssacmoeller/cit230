Rafting Bear River

by Alyssa Moeller

When the temperatures start to rise and you're looking for a fun way to stay cool, look no further than the Bear River in Preston, Idaho. With a variety of floating options for all skill levels, it's the perfect place for summer fun. Float on a tube, go for a whitewater ride, or kayak your way downstream!
